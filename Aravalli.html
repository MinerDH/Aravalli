About the Aravalli Range

The Aravalli Range is one of the oldest mountain ranges in the world, stretching across Rajasthan, Haryana, Delhi, and Gujarat. Known for its ancient rocky hills and dry landscapes, the Aravallis play a vital role in protecting the region from desert expansion and preserving ecological balance.
